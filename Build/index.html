where + when

Summer on the Cape? Yes, please. Love Yoga Fest will take place August 14-17, 2014 in Hyannis, MA. Centrally located at the heart of the sandbar, Hyannis is easily accessible by bus, CapeFlyer from Boston and ferry from the islands, as well as air (Boston or Hyannis). Come for the weekend or extend your vacation and enjoy all that the Cape has to offer. More information to help you plan your trip will be available soon.

what

The Love Yoga Festival is a celebration of all things yoga and wellness for yogis of all levels. The fest will feature a fun welcome evening followed by two full days of yoga, body and energy work, and unique experiences … plus, we have exciting surprises to reveal in the coming weeks! We are thrilled to be bringing you some well-known names in the yoga community as well as favorite instructors from throughout New England, including Cape Cod.

who

Inspired by the incredible New England yoga community and the stunning natural environment Cape Cod offers, four local yogis came together to dream up a yoga festival that celebrates both. Former yoga studio owner Annika Iliadis, eco-boutique owner Amanda Converse, artist and gallery owner Jen Villa, and communications consultant Kate Sheehan are excited to share Love Yoga Fest with kindred spirits from near and far.
